**Meet ClassicPress: No Gutenberg. Great Future!**

Version 1 of ClassicPress will be a Gutenberg free experience of WordPress, compatible with your existing site. Version 2 will be community led ‚Äì features will be brought up and voted for by the community. If you always wanted to have a say but felt your voice was never heard **NOW** is your chance to make ClassicPress **your** CMS!

For more information, see:

- [The official ClassicPress website](https://www.classicpress.net/)
- [Suggest features](https://petitions.classicpress.net/)
- [The ClassicPress Democracy](https://www.classicpress.net/democracy/)
- [This repository's open issues](https://github.com/ClassicPress/ClassicPress/issues)
